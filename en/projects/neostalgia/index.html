Interactive XR x Generative AI BIFAN Selection MSIT Minister Citation

Neostalgia

An XR exhibition system that interviews visitors about memory, generates visual assets from the conversation, and keeps the whole experience operable through logging and a dashboard.

Whisper GPT Skybox AI Meshy Dashboard

Role

Connected the Unity experience loop, AI pipeline, session logging, and operating dashboard into one exhibition-ready flow.

Top Feature 01

A memory interview orchestrator where visitor gestures, voice input, and generated results decide the next state of the XR experience.

Top Feature 02

A voice-to-AI conversation pipeline using VAD recording, STT, GPT function-calling, nickname extraction, and emotion-aware response handling.

Top Feature 03

A generative asset pipeline that turns conversation logs into skyboxes and 3D objects that can be experienced inside XR.

Outcome

Neostalgia matters because it is not just an AI-connected XR prototype. It earned BIFAN XR Beyond Reality official selection, received the MSIT Minister Citation, and operated in front of real exhibition traffic.

The project reached beyond content generation: it included telemetry, cloud logging, asset retrieval, and an operations dashboard for looking back at sessions.

Operations scale: 130+ direct participants, six exhibition rounds, and roughly 2,000 visitors across the event footprint.
Why it is senior-level: the hard part was orchestration: handling async generation, visitor flow, XR timing, and operational observability together.
Roles: interactive XR, creative AI systems, exhibition technology, and applied AI experience.
