# Cover Letter (English)

Dear Hiring Team,

I am a senior Unity/XR systems engineer with 10 years of experience building real-time software across industrial XR, BIM runtime tooling, robotics control interfaces, and applied AI systems. The common thread in my work is practical ownership. I care about whether a system can survive real operators, delivery pressure, validation, maintenance, and repeated use in the field.

My projects have consistently moved beyond isolated prototypes. In **VR Robot Teleoperation**, I connected Quest 3 input, Unity runtime logic, a Flask bridge, RoboDK, safety handling, and in-house Jacobian IK work into a usable XR control pipeline. In **WatchBIM**, I built heavy-geometry interaction features such as a custom BIM hit engine, cross-section generation, a 2D analysis viewer, and tool-grade camera controls for large infrastructure data. In **DXCenter**, I focused on authoring structure and operator usability, building an editor environment that reduced repeated setup work and gave non-developers a clearer runtime workflow.

More recently, I have been extending the same mindset into AI systems. In **Neostalgia**, I designed and connected a real exhibition-ready pipeline across Whisper, GPT, TTS, Skybox AI, Meshy, XR timing, and an operations dashboard. In **Multi-Agent Orchestration**, I developed reusable operating rules for role separation, review gates, launcher flow, and collaboration structure. In **Ops-Cure**, I built a Discord-native local agent operations system with a NAS bridge, Windows launcher, markdown workspaces, and artifact-aware session tracking. What matters to me is not adding AI for novelty, but designing systems that remain observable, governable, and useful under real operating conditions.

I believe my strongest fit is in teams that need someone who can bridge implementation depth and delivery reality: industrial XR, simulation or digital-twin software, robotics interfaces, BIM or geometry-heavy tooling, and applied AI products where workflow structure matters as much as the model itself. I am most effective when I can take responsibility for both the technical core and the practical constraints around it.

Thank you for your time and consideration. I would be glad to discuss how my experience could support your team and the specific problems you are solving.

Sincerely,
